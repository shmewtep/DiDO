# Ontology Requirements

## Goal & Purpose
* **Goal:** To represent dialogue structure and pragmatic attributes of conversation.
* **Purpose:** This ontology is being developed as part of a PhD project to automate annotation of dialogue datasets and extract semantic triples from dialogue transcripts.

## Scope & Boundaries
* **In-Scope:** Dialogue structure, dialogue acts and pragmatics, provenance, dialogue datasets.
* **Out-of-Scope:** Prosody, nonverbal cues.

## Requirements Specification
### Non-Functional Requirements
* **NFR-1:** Implementation in OWL 2.

## Knowledge Resources to Reuse
* **Top & Middle Level Ontology:** [Semanticscience Integrated Ontology (SIO)](https://github.com/MaastrichtU-IDS/semanticscience)
* **Domain Ontologies:** [Time Ontology in OWL (owl-time)](https://www.w3.org/TR/owl-time/), [Data Catalog Vocabulary (DCAT)](https://www.w3.org/TR/vocab-dcat-3/)
* **Provenance:** [The PROV Ontology (PROV-O)](https://www.w3.org/TR/prov-o/)
